Lead routing control room: the 4-layer operating model

March 24, 2026 • Operations

Most teams lose leads in routing, not marketing. The control-room model separates intake, qualification, assignment, and owner visibility so no request gets stuck.

  • Layer 1: intake normalization (channels and minimum fields).
  • Layer 2: qualification score (urgency, fit, expected value).
  • Layer 3: assignment logic (owner/team/SLA).
  • Layer 4: escalation and audit trail.
