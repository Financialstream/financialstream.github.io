AI SOP rollout checklist: from pilot to weekly operations

March 22, 2026 • SOP

The common failure is launching automation before SOP ownership is defined. This checklist keeps rollout stable and measurable.

  • Define one owner and fallback owner.
  • Lock required inputs and stop conditions.
  • Set escalation routes for edge cases.
  • Track 4 metrics weekly: response speed, misses, rework, conversion.
  • Run review every Friday and patch one bottleneck at a time.
