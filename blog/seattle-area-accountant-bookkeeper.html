Sales tax is one of the easiest places for a small business to get surprised: rates change, rules differ by state, and filing calendars don’t wait. In 2026, the practical “change” for most owners isn’t one new federal sales‑tax law (the U.S. doesn’t have a single federal sales tax), but the ongoing reality of state and local rate updates and the need to keep your bookkeeping clean enough to file accurately.

High‑intent questions people ask in Seattle

Common search queries include: “Seattle bookkeeper”, “accountant Seattle”, “QuickBooks bookkeeper Seattle”, and “tax preparation Seattle”. People usually want speed, clarity, and no surprises at tax time.

The fastest way to start is a short call: we clarify your business type, your state, whether you use QuickBooks, and what’s most urgent (cleanup, monthly bookkeeping, sales tax, payroll, or tax returns).

What to prepare before the first call

Have basic details ready: entity type (LLC / sole proprietor), your state of registration, bank/credit card accounts, and whether you already use QuickBooks Online.

If you have backlog, estimate how many months are behind. If you track sales tax or have employees, mention that up front.

Monthly bookkeeping that actually helps

Good bookkeeping is not only ‘entering transactions’. It includes consistent categorization, reconciliations, and basic checks so your reports are reliable.

Each month you should be able to read your Profit & Loss and Balance Sheet with confidence.

Sales tax and payroll: when they matter

If you sell taxable goods/services, sales tax tracking and filing cadence should match your bookkeeping so you don’t scramble before deadlines.

If you have employees, payroll should be reconciled monthly so your books match payroll reports and tax deposits.

Remote support across the U.S. (Seattle area friendly)

Remote bookkeeping is standard in the U.S. today — it’s often faster than in‑person because document sharing and monthly routines are streamlined.

Seattle/Washington clients often like the time‑zone alignment, but we support clients nationwide.
