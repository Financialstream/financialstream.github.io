If you’re searching for Seattle bookkeeper or QuickBooks bookkeeping Seattle, you usually want one thing: clean books that are reliable for decisions and tax time. The most effective approach is a simple monthly routine: consistent categorization, reconciliations, and clear reporting.

What to prepare (5 minutes)

  • Business or individual (or both).
  • Location: Seattle / WA (and any other states involved).
  • Your current bookkeeping: QuickBooks / Excel / bank only / receipts.
  • How many months need catch‑up (if any).

What “good monthly bookkeeping” means

  • Stable categorization rules (so reports don’t change every month).
  • Monthly reconciliations for banks and credit cards.
  • Cleanup of common issues (duplicates, personal transactions, misc categories).
  • Short monthly summary: what matters to the owner.
