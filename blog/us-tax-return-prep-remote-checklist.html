Tax return preparation for U.S. clients (remote): checklist & process

Updated: March 2026

← Back to Updates

For remote U.S. tax return preparation, the fastest path is a clear document checklist and early issue spotting. This guide helps you organize the right documents before filing so we can confirm scope, timeline, and next steps quickly.

Quick checklist

  • State(s) where you lived, worked, or had business activity during the tax year.
  • Income forms: W‑2, 1099, business docs if applicable.
  • Expenses (self‑employed/business): bank statements, receipts, categories.
  • If you received IRS/state notices, mention it in your description (we avoid estimating penalties in chat).

How to start

If scope isn’t clear yet, start with a free 15‑minute consultation to confirm your situation and next steps.
