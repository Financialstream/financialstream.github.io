QuickBooks Online bookkeeping: monthly close checklist (U.S.)

Updated: March 2026

← Back to Updates

If you use QuickBooks Online for monthly bookkeeping, the goal is simple: close each month on time with clean reconciliations and clear reports. This checklist helps U.S. small businesses keep books accurate before tax season and lender reviews.

Quick checklist

  • Closing period and legal entities in scope (single business or multiple entities).
  • Bank and credit card feeds connected, reviewed, and categorized.
  • Open AR/AP reviewed, duplicate transactions removed, uncategorized items resolved.
  • Month-end package prepared: reconciliations, P&L, balance sheet, and notes for unusual items.
