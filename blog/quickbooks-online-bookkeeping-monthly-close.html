Clean books are built monthly. This guide gives a practical QuickBooks Online (QBO) monthly close checklist and common errors to avoid.

Who this is for

  • U.S. small businesses using QuickBooks Online
  • Owners who want predictable monthly reporting
  • Teams preparing for tax filing or lender requests

Monthly close checklist

  1. Import/confirm bank and credit card transactions
  2. Categorize expenses consistently
  3. Match transfers and remove duplicates
  4. Reconcile accounts
  5. Review P&L and Balance Sheet for anomalies

Common bookkeeping errors

  • Mixing personal and business transactions
  • Not reconciling monthly
  • Incorrectly categorizing contractor payments or taxes
