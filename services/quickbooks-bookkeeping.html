QuickBooks Bookkeeping (Monthly)

Monthly bookkeeping that keeps your records clean and tax-ready, built around QuickBooks Online and a clear, repeatable monthly close.

Back to main page

What you get

  • Monthly categorization aligned to your business
  • Bank and credit-card reconciliations
  • Cleanup and catch-up bookkeeping when needed
  • Monthly reports (P&L, Balance Sheet) with notes and follow-ups

Who this is for

Small businesses and self-employed owners who want clean books, predictable reporting, and fewer surprises at year-end. We support Washington clients, including the Seattle area, and businesses across the U.S.

When you usually need this service

This service is usually the right fit when your books are falling behind, transactions are being posted inconsistently, reconciliations are missing, or year-end tax preparation feels harder than it should. It is also a strong fit if you already use QuickBooks Online but want a more reliable monthly close and cleaner reports.

Many clients come to us after trying to manage bookkeeping internally for a while, after switching from another bookkeeper, or right before tax filing when they realize the books are not fully ready.

How we work (remote)

We set up a secure workflow for documents and access. Most communication happens by email or phone, and you get consistent monthly results without visiting an office.

What to prepare before starting

  • QuickBooks Online access, if you already have an account
  • Recent bank and credit-card statements for the periods that need review
  • A short note on whether this is ongoing monthly bookkeeping, cleanup, catch-up, or a mix of these
  • Any payroll, sales tax, or owner-transaction details that affect the books

If you are not sure what applies, that is fine. A short first review is usually enough to confirm scope and the cleanest next step.
