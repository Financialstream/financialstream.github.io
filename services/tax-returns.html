For individuals and small businesses

Whether you are a small business owner, contractor, or W-2 employee with additional income, we help you keep records in order and avoid last-minute stress.

When you usually need this service

This service is usually the right fit when tax season is approaching and documents are still scattered, when business and personal records need to be separated more clearly, or when year-end bookkeeping is not fully aligned with filing requirements.

It is also a common fit after a major business change, a backlog in bookkeeping, new contractor income, or a year in which several income streams need to be reviewed together before filing.

What to prepare before starting

  • Your prior return, if available
  • Current-year tax forms and income records already received
  • A short note on whether this is a business return, personal return, or both
  • Any bookkeeping, payroll, or sales-tax issues that may affect filing

If some documents are still missing, we can still start with a first review and clarify what is most important to gather next.
