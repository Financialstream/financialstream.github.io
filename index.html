Client intake & communication automation (example)

A practical guided flow with human control: fewer repetitive questions, faster responses, structured data and document collection.

Channels (priority)
  • Website AI chat — primary entry point for new inquiries.
  • Messenger bot (WhatsApp/Telegram) — same flow in a familiar channel.
  • Phone + SMS — missed-lead recovery, triage, optional voice layer.
Step-by-step flow
  • Route to the right branch: service type, urgency, new vs existing client.
  • Ask 3–7 key questions and generate a branch-specific checklist.
  • Provide the right form/portal link (portal ≠ “mobile app”) for upload/sign/pay.
  • Send the owner a structured summary and the next step.
  • Hand off to a human for uncertainty, edge cases, or sensitive topics.
Measurable impact
  • Faster response time and routing.
  • Fewer missed leads and less “back-and-forth for documents”.
  • Significantly less owner time spent repeating the same instructions.
