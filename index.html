FAQ

Common questions — answered simply.

How do we get started?

Send us a message or call with a short overview of what you need (bookkeeping, taxes, catch‑up, formation/licensing). We’ll ask a couple of quick questions and send clear next‑step instructions.

Do I need to come to your office?

No. We work remotely and by phone, so an in‑person visit isn’t required. If a meeting is needed, it can be scheduled by appointment.

.

Do you work with QuickBooks Online?

Yes. We work in QuickBooks Online and can help set up access and a clean monthly workflow.

.

Can you clean up backlogged or messy books?

Yes. We can reconcile and correct prior periods so your books are accurate and you can move forward with a predictable routine.

.

Do you help with sales tax and state revenue filings?

Yes. We’ll confirm what applies in your state and help with filings and related questions.

.

Do you handle payroll and quarterly reporting?

Yes. We can support payroll and quarterly filings. If you already have a system, we’ll align to it; if not, we’ll recommend a simple setup.

.

Do you prepare and file tax returns (business & personal)?

Yes. We support both business and individual tax returns. We’ll confirm your situation and documents first, then agree on scope and timeline.

.

How much does it cost — and how fast do you respond?

Pricing depends on transaction volume and complexity (taxes, payroll, filings). After a short contact, we’ll provide a clear estimate. We respond as quickly as possible during business hours and will set expectations when a review is needed.
