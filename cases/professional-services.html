Professional & Financial Services

Accounting, tax, legal, real estate, advisory — document-heavy, regulated, trust-driven workflows.

Typical problems

  • Inbox overload and missed follow-ups impact client retention.
  • Unstructured requests cause delays and errors.
  • Document collection and routing is often manual.

Example system architecture

  • Email triage + client routing (new vs existing, service type, urgency).
  • AI-assisted drafting with guardrails (no invented numbers, ask clarifying questions).
  • Structured intake forms + checklist automation for document completeness.
  • Operational log: status, next action owner, deadlines.

Client intake & communication automation (practical flow)

  • Multi-branch intake: route by service type, urgency, and client status.
  • Primary channel: website AI chat; optional messenger and phone/SMS layer.
  • 3–7 key questions → checklist → the right form/portal link (portal ≠ “app”).
  • Owner summary + operational log (status, next action owner, deadlines).
  • Guardrails: no invented numbers; ask clarifying questions; human handoff for edge cases.

Typical measurable impact

  • Lower missed follow-ups and improved response SLA.
  • Cleaner client onboarding and fewer back-and-forth messages.
  • More predictable operations during peak season.
