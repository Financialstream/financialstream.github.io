High-Volume Service Businesses

Contractors, home services, agencies — speed-to-lead and routing quality determine revenue.

Typical problems

  • Slow response time loses deals to faster competitors.
  • Leads arrive via multiple channels with inconsistent data.
  • Teams lack a single source of truth for follow-ups.

Example system architecture

  • Unified lead capture: calls/SMS/forms/inbox into a single pipeline.
  • AI qualification: intent, budget signals, location, urgency.
  • Routing rules: by service line, geo, availability, priority.
  • Automated follow-up sequences with STOP compliance and logging.

Typical measurable impact

  • Faster speed-to-lead and reduced lead leakage.
  • More consistent qualification and handoff quality.
  • Clear metrics for marketing and ops alignment.
