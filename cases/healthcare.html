Healthcare & Clinics

Dental, medical, aesthetic, diagnostic networks — multi-location operations and sensitive customer experience.

Typical problems

  • Missed calls and delayed responses reduce booked appointments.
  • Manual intake creates inconsistency and staff overload.
  • Scheduling handoffs are fragmented across channels.

Example system architecture

  • Inbound layer: call/SMS/email capture + routing to the right team.
  • AI-assisted intake: collect intent, urgency, insurance/eligibility basics (if applicable).
  • Human approval where needed + escalation for edge cases.
  • Logging + KPI dashboard: response time, booked vs lost, follow-up SLA.

Typical measurable impact

  • Faster response time and clearer routing.
  • Higher conversion from inbound inquiries to booked visits.
  • Reduced manual coordination for front desk teams.
