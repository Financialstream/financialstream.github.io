Client Intake & Communication Automation

A guided, human-controlled flow that reduces repeated questions, speeds up response, and turns inquiries into structured next steps.

What this solves

  • Missed calls and slow replies that lose leads.
  • Repeated questions and manual onboarding that consumes owner time.
  • Document collection chaos (wrong files, missing info, endless follow-ups).
  • Clients confused by “apps” and portals — unclear steps and too many screens.

Channels (priority order)

  • Website AI chat — primary entry point and best UX control.
  • Messenger bot (WhatsApp/Telegram) — same flow in a familiar channel.
  • Phone + SMS — missed-call recovery, routing, optional voice layer.

How it works (guided flow)

  • Triage and route into the right branch (service type, urgency, new vs existing).
  • Ask 3–7 key questions and generate a checklist for that branch.
  • Send the right form/portal link for upload/sign/pay (portal ≠ “app”).
  • Capture contact details and create a structured intake record.
  • Offer booking via calendar integration when appropriate.
  • Notify the owner with a concise summary and next action.
  • Human handoff for uncertainty, edge cases, or sensitive topics.

Control & guardrails

  • Human-in-the-loop by design: escalation rules and clear ownership.
  • No invented prices/dates/commitments; ask clarifying questions when uncertain.
  • Logging and audit trail for every step (who/what/when).

What we measure

  • Speed-to-lead (time to first useful response).
  • Missed-lead recovery rate (calls/SMS/inbox follow-up).
  • Time saved on onboarding and document collection.
  • Conversion to booking / next step.
