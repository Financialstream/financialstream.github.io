Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-08

Financial Stream LLC respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information we collect

  • Contact details you submit (for example, name, phone number, email address, and message).
  • Business details you voluntarily share to request bookkeeping, tax, or administrative support.
  • Basic technical data (such as pages visited) for site functionality and security.

How we use information

  • To respond to requests and provide service-related support.
  • To deliver service updates and reminders by email or SMS when you request or consent to that communication.
  • To improve our service quality and maintain site security.

SMS communications (service-related)

If you opt in, we may send service-related SMS only at your request (for example, after a missed call, to confirm a consultation, or to share status updates). We do not send marketing blasts. Message frequency varies by situation. Carrier rates may apply.

You can opt out at any time by replying STOP. For help, reply HELP.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your phone number with third parties for their marketing. We may use trusted service providers (for example, messaging and hosting providers) only to operate our services, under confidentiality obligations.

Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Your choices

  • Request access, correction, or deletion of information you submitted through our site.
  • Opt out of SMS by replying STOP; request help by replying HELP.

Contact

To contact us about privacy, please use the Contact form on our website.
