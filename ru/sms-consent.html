SMS Consent & Messaging Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-15

Financial Stream LLC may send service-related SMS only with consent and only for customer-initiated communication.

1) Program description

  • Customer-initiated, service-related messaging only (no marketing).
  • Typical reasons: responding to an inquiry, missed call follow-up, scheduling, status updates.
  • Message frequency varies.

2) How you opt in

You opt in by checking the SMS consent box on our website contact form, or by requesting SMS communication during a call or conversation.

Our missed-call auto-reply sends one SMS after you call us and we miss your call. Replying continues the conversation.

3) Opt out / help

Reply STOP to opt out at any time. Reply HELP for help.

Msg & data rates may apply.

4) Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact Financial Stream LLC.
