SMS Consent & Messaging Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-15

1) Program description

Financial Stream LLC is an accounting / bookkeeping / tax services firm. We use SMS only for customer-initiated, service-related communication (no marketing). Typical reasons include responding to a client inquiry, a missed call follow-up, appointment scheduling, and status updates.

2) How you opt in

We may message you only if one of the following applies:

  • Missed call follow-up: You call our published business phone number and the call is missed. We may send one SMS acknowledging the missed call and inviting you to reply if you need assistance.
  • Website request (optional): You submit our website contact form and check the SMS consent box to request SMS communication.

You can opt out anytime by replying STOP. For help, reply HELP. Message and data rates may apply.

3) Message frequency

Message frequency varies by your activity. For missed calls, we typically send one follow-up SMS. Additional messages occur only if you reply and continue a 1:1 conversation.

4) Example messages

Missed call auto-reply (one-time):

Financial Stream LLC: We received your call but missed you. Reply with your question and we’ll get back to you. Reply STOP to opt out, HELP for help. Msg & data rates may apply.

Customer reply (your message):

Hi, I need help with bookkeeping for my LLC. Can we talk today?

5) No marketing / no sharing

We do not send promotional or marketing SMS and we do not sell your phone number. We use your information only to provide requested services and support.

6) Consent records

For compliance, we keep basic records showing how consent was obtained, such as: (a) call logs indicating an inbound call and a missed-call follow-up message, and/or (b) website form submissions with an SMS consent checkbox selection (timestamp and submitted phone number).

7) Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact us via the website contact form or by calling our business number.
