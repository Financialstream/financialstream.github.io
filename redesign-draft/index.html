    Monthly close process

    A monthly close process that keeps your books moving.

    Instead of waiting until tax season, we organize the work into repeatable monthly steps — from documents and transactions to reconciliations, reports, and tax-ready records.

    1

    Collect documents and access

    We confirm what is needed, request the right access, and create a clear document workflow before the work begins.

    2

    Categorize transactions

    Transactions are reviewed and categorized consistently so QuickBooks becomes easier to understand and maintain.

    3

    Reconcile accounts

    Bank and credit card activity is matched and reconciled so reports are based on cleaner records.

    4

    Review exceptions

    Missing items, unclear transactions, and questions are flagged instead of being ignored or guessed.

    Prepare reports

    Financial documents need a careful workflow.

    Accounting work often involves private business and tax information. We keep the process organized with clear checklists, careful document exchange, and access requested only when it is needed for the work.

    • No passwords or SSNs through open text messages
    • Access requested only when needed
    • Questions and missing items tracked clearly
    • Communication in English or Russian
