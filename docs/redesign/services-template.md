# Services Template (Planning Only)

## Standard Service Page Structure
1. H1
2. Short promise
3. Who it is for
4. When you need it
5. What is included
6. What is not included
7. How the process works
8. What to prepare
9. Timeline / cadence
10. Pricing or scope orientation
11. FAQ
12. Related services
13. Related resources
14. CTA

## Example: Bookkeeping Page

### H1
QuickBooks Bookkeeping for Small Businesses

### Subtitle
Monthly categorization, reconciliations, and reports so your books stay clean and tax-ready.

### Included
- Monthly transaction categorization
- Bank and credit card reconciliation
- QuickBooks review
- P&L and Balance Sheet
- Notes on missing or unclear items
- Year-end tax-ready records

### Not Included Unless Added
- Payroll
- Sales tax filing
- Tax return preparation
- 1099 filing
- Accounts payable / accounts receivable
- Inventory management
